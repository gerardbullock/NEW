The application of statistics and modeling tools to create predictions about future outcomes and performance is referred to as predictive analytics. Predictive analytics examines current and past data patterns to evaluate whether they are likely to reappear. This enables businesses and investors to reallocate their resources in order to capitalize on potential future events. Predictive analytics can also be utilized to boost operational efficiencies and lower risk.
